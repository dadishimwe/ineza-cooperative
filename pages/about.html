About INEZA Cooperative

Empowering women through community and craftsmanship.

Our Story

The story of INEZA Cooperative is one of strength, courage, and community. All the women of INEZA Cooperative are living with HIV/AIDS as a result of genocidal rape and sexual violence. However, rather than focusing on the horrors of the genocide and its aftermath, the women of INEZA Cooperative are a testament to the power of Rwandan women to work toward a better future for themselves, their families, and their nation.
